A leader self-assessment grounded in the Radical Candor framework

Great teams are built on trust and honest communication — but most leaders don't have a clear picture of where their team actually stands. This assessment gives you that picture.

You'll get two scores: one for how you're showing up as a leader, and one for how you perceive your team's health across six key dimensions. Both come from your inputs. It's a starting point, not a verdict.

How this works: You answer all questions — some ask about your own behavior, others ask what you observe or believe about your team. Both scores come from your inputs alone. At the end, you'll have the option to schedule a personalized call with a Radical Candor coach to walk through your results and talk about what to do next. If you want to go deeper, you can also explore a team assessment — where your direct reports weigh in — separately.
